# 🏢 Stage 10 — Negotiation & Finalization

## 🎯 What the Stage Is For

To finalize:

> Scope, pricing, and contractual terms

---

## 👥 Who Is Involved

### Customer Side

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Procurement | Leads negotiation process |
| Legal | Reviews contractual terms and compliance |
| Finance | Validates financial aspects and commitments |
| IT Leadership | Ensures alignment with technical and operational expectations |

---

### Service Provider Side

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Sales | Drives negotiation and deal closure |
| Legal | Reviews and finalizes contract terms |
| Finance | Ensures pricing and margin alignment |
| Delivery Leadership | Validates feasibility of commitments |

---

## 🛠 What Gets Produced

| Output | Description |
|--------|------------|
| Final Contract | Legally binding agreement between client and vendor |
| Statement of Work (SOW) | Detailed scope, deliverables, and responsibilities |

---

## ⚠️ What Can Go Wrong

| Risk | Impact |
|------|--------|
| Scope ambiguity | Leads to future disputes |
| Aggressive pricing | Creates delivery pressure and risk |
| Misaligned expectations | Causes execution challenges and dissatisfaction |
