# 🏢 Stage 8 — RFP Approval & Release

## 🎯 Goal

Ensure RFP is:

- Complete 
- Risk-free
- Legally sound
- Ready for formal vendor engagement

---

## 👥 Who Is Involved

- CIO / CTO
- Procurement
- Legal
- Risk & Compliance

---

## 🛠 What Happens

| Function | Activity |
|----------|----------|
| Legal | Reviews liability clauses and penalties |
| Risk Team | Reviews vendor dependency risks |
| IT | Validates technical completeness |

---

## 📦 Output

1. RFP is Approved
2. RFP released to shortlisted vendors
3. Timelines shared
4. Q&A window (vendors can ask clarification questions)
5. Submission deadline

At this Stage : Vendors now start preparing proposals
