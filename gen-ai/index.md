---

A structured learning series covering:
- LLMs
- Transformers
- Embeddings
- RAG
- Agentic Systems

## Philosophy

This series focuses on:
- first-principles understanding,
- systems thinking,
- architectural reasoning,
- and durable mental models.

Not hype.  
Not shallow tutorials.  
Not framework chasing.

## Core Mental Model

> Traditional software executes explicit logic.  
> Modern AI systems probabilistically operate over unstructured knowledge spaces.

That shift changes everything.
